Guidelines & Rules

Please read all instructions carefully before submitting your registration

Registration Guidelines

  • Each team must contain a minimum of 2 members and a maximum of 4 members.
  • All team members must provide valid details such as name, department, college, email, and WhatsApp number.
  • The team leader is responsible for submitting the final registration form.
  • Registration will be confirmed only after successful fee payment and screenshot upload.
  • Please keep the transaction ID safe for future verification.
  • One team should submit only one registration form.

Hackathon Rules

  • The hackathon duration is 24 hours from 01-04-2026 to 02-04-2026.
  • Participants must carry their college ID card during reporting.
  • Teams must develop their project during the hackathon duration only.
  • Use of AI tools is encouraged, but plagiarism or copied projects are strictly not allowed.
  • Judges’ decisions regarding evaluation and prizes will be final.
  • Any form of misconduct may lead to direct disqualification.

Submission & Participation Rules

  • Teams must present a working prototype, demo, or project output at the end of the event.
  • Participants should bring their own laptops, chargers, and required accessories.
  • Internet and basic event support will be provided as per availability.
  • Participants must join the official WhatsApp group after successful registration for event updates.
  • Organizers have the right to make minor schedule changes if required.
  • By registering, participants agree to follow all instructions communicated by the organizing committee.
